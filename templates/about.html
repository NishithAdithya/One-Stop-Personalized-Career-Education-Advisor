✨ About EduPath

Empowering the next generation of students to make informed career decisions

🎯 Our Mission

EduPath is dedicated to revolutionizing career guidance for Class 10 and 12 students in India. We believe every student deserves access to personalized, data-driven career counseling to help them choose the right stream, degree program, and educational institution.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between student potential and career clarity, ensuring that no talented student makes career choices out of confusion or lack of information.

🚀 Our Vision

To become India's most trusted career guidance platform, helping millions of students unlock their full potential and build successful, fulfilling careers.

❓ Why This Matters

Every year, thousands of Class 10 and 12 students face a critical decision: which stream should I choose? This decision impacts the next decade of their academic and professional life. Yet, many students make this choice based on:

  • Peer pressure or family expectations
  • Limited information about career options
  • Lack of proper aptitude assessment
  • No guidance on college selection

EduPath solves this by providing a science-backed aptitude quiz, personalized recommendations, and clear pathways to success.

🔄 How It Works

Step 1: Assessment

Take our quick 12-question aptitude quiz that evaluates your interests, strengths, and preferences.

Step 2: Analysis

Our intelligent algorithm analyzes your responses and calculates your aptitude score for each stream.

Step 3: Recommendations

Receive personalized recommendations including streams, degrees, careers, and relevant exams.

Step 4: Exploration

Use our resources to explore colleges, connect with mentors, and plan your next steps.

💻 Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript
  • Database: MySQL/PostgreSQL (scalable)
  • Hosting: Cloud-based infrastructure
